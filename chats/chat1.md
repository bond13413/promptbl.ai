## User

is wordmark the logos?

## Assistant

Close — they're related but not the same:

- **Mark** = the glyph/symbol alone (the cursor block, the chevron, etc.) — works as a favicon or app icon.
- **Wordmark** = the brand name *set as type* (`promptbl.ai` in JetBrains Mono).
- **Logo / lockup** = the full assembled thing — often **mark + wordmark together** (e.g. `>_ promptbl.ai▮`).

So in your canvas:
- "**Mark variations**" artboard = 6 glyph options
- "**Wordmark variants**" artboard = 6 ways to set the *name* (with/without prompt prefix, stacked, bracketed, etc.)
- The **canonical logo** is wordmark variant **A** — `>_ promptbl.ai▮` — that's what you use as "the logo" in most places.
