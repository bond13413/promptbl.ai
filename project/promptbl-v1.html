How it works

Three steps from
brief to reporting.

We've spent two years wiring sponsored-placement inventory directly into the LLM surfaces marketers care about. You buy it the way you'd buy any other media — and you get the receipts.

STEP 01

Brief the buy.

Audience, intents, geos, KPI. Upload a CSV of seed prompts or let our targeting engine cluster them for you.

$ promptbl new --audience "B2B founders"
$ promptbl prompts upload seeds.csv
→ clustered 1,284 intents into 28 prompt-sets
→ projected reach: 4.2M MAU
STEP 02

Place it everywhere
at once.

One creative, one budget. We syndicate across every LLM surface in the network — and pause where it doesn't pay.

14 surfaces · 28 prompt-sets · 1 dashboard
STEP 03

Read the receipts.

Real-time placement, impression and click logs. IAB-aligned. Plug into your existing MMP or pipe to BigQuery.
